# Power-BI-Dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard that provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance for a retail superstore. The dashboard consolidates key business metrics into a single, visually intuitive view to support data-driven decision-making across regions, product categories, and customer segments. 🛒📈

✨ Key Features
💰 KPI Summary Cards — At-a-glance metrics for Total Sales (1.57M), Total Profit (175.26K), Average Delivery Days (23K), and Total Quantity Sold (22K).
🗺️ Geographic Sales Map — Visualizes Sum of Sales and Sum of Profit by U.S. state to identify high- and low-performing regions.
🎚️ Regional Filters — Interactive slicers for Central, East, South, and West regions.
📉 Sales & Profit Trends — Month-over-month and year-over-year (2019 vs 2020) line charts tracking profit and sales performance.
📦 Category & Sub-Category Breakdown — Bar charts highlighting top 3 performing sub-categories (Phones, Chairs, Binders) and overall sales by category (Office Supplies, Technology, Furniture).
👥 Segment Analysis — Donut charts breaking down sales by customer segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office), payment mode (COD, Online, Cards), and shipping method (Standard, Second, First, Same Day).

🛠️ Tech Stack
🔷 Tool: Microsoft Power BI
🗂️ Data Source: Superstore Sales dataset (CSV/Excel)
📊 Visuals: KPI cards, filled map, line charts, bar charts, donut charts, slicers
💡 Insights Delivered

This dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly assess:

🌎 Which regions and states drive the most sales and profit
📅 Seasonal and yearly sales trends
🏆 Top-performing product categories and sub-categories
🚚 Customer preferences in shipping and payment methods
📦 Overall order volume through quantity sold
